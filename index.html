About Us

The Bapatla Regional Revival Society is dedicated to transforming Bapatla into a vibrant hub of education, culture, and sustainable development.

Our Vision

To revive and reimagine Bapatla as a vibrant hub of education, culture, innovation, and sustainable regional development rooted in its heritage.

Our Mission

  • Reinvigorate education and research
  • Promote entrepreneurship and livelihoods
  • Improve infrastructure and environment
  • Foster art, culture, and community engagement
